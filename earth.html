Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only one known to support life. Its oxygen-rich atmosphere and magnetic field protect it from harmful solar radiation. Water covers 71% of its surface, playing a crucial role in regulating climate. The planet experiences plate tectonics, which reshape its surface over time. Earth’s tilted axis (23.5°) creates the seasons, while the Moon stabilizes its rotation. The biosphere has supported life for at least 3.5 billion years.
